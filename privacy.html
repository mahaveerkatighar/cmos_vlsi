Privacy Policy for CMOS VLSI DESIGN SYNOPSIS

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Welcome to CMOS VLSI DESIGN SYNOPSIS, a web study dashboard designed by Mahaveer Katighar. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information collected through your interaction with our application. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices in connection with the Google OAuth and YouTube integration services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Google Sign-In feature on CMOS VLSI DESIGN SYNOPSIS, we collect certain profile details directly via Google APIs with your explicit consent:

  • Basic Profile Information: We access your Google account name, given name, and profile picture URL. This is used exclusively to display your profile in the header and personalize the student comment board.
  • YouTube Permissions: If you grant access to connect your account, our application can interact with YouTube APIs on your behalf to fetch and post comments directly on the actual video threads. We do not store or download your private YouTube account data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information solely to power the learning dashboard features:

  • To display your student profile card in the top navigation panel.
  • To fetch, display, and publish active study comments directly on the real YouTube lesson video pages under your Google username.
  • To log and track your progress through the 16 masterclass video list.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your credentials and access tokens are never transmitted to or stored on our servers. All OAuth authorization tokens are cached directly in your local browser's localStorage for session persistence and will automatically expire or be deleted when you click "Sign Out". Your comments are saved directly to public YouTube comments threads, hosted securely by Google.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application interfaces with YouTube API Services. By using CMOS VLSI DESIGN SYNOPSIS, you also agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy. You can manage and revoke our app's access to your Google account at any time through your Google Security Settings page.
