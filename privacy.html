Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Mahaveer Katighar VLSI Course Dashboard

1. Overview

This privacy policy explains how the CMOS VLSI Design Synopsis course dashboard ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. The App is operated by Mahaveer Katighar for educational purposes.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Data: When you sign in with Google, we access your name, email address, and profile picture via Google OAuth 2.0. This is used to personalise your experience.
  • YouTube Data: With your permission, we use the YouTube Data API v3 to like videos and read comments on your behalf. We only access what is necessary for the features you use.
  • Course Progress: Your watched videos, liked videos, and notes are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you are signed in.
  • Usage Logs: If you are signed in, basic session data (current video, progress, branch) may be synced to a secure Firebase Realtime Database for the instructor's admin console.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalise your course experience with your name and avatar.
  • To like YouTube videos on your behalf when you tap the Like button.
  • To verify your YouTube subscription status.
  • To load and display YouTube comments for the active lesson.
  • To allow the instructor to monitor student attendance and course progress.

4. Google API Services

The App uses Google OAuth 2.0 with the following scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.force-ssl — to like videos and read/post comments on YouTube on your behalf.

The App's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • OAuth access tokens are stored in sessionStorage only and are never persisted beyond your current browser session.
  • Course progress is stored in localStorage on your device.
  • We do not sell your data to any third parties.

6. Your Rights

You can sign out at any time using the Google account pill in the top navigation. You can clear all local data by clearing your browser's site data for this page.
